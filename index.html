Orientation Chair

I am currently the Orientation Chair, also known as 'High Tech', for The Queen's School of Computing Orientation Week 2015.

As Orientation Chair I currently:

  • Manage the eight person Computing Orientation Committee and my team of numerous Orientation Leaders; which I have hired to ensure the smooth running, planning and execution of the School of Computing's Orientation Week 2015.
  • Provide a safe and supportive environment for incoming students.
  • Ensure all Orientation activities promote and foster a positive relationship with the Queen’s and Kingston Communities.
  • Am responsible for all communications with incoming students prior to Orientation Week.
  • Am the Speaker for all Computing Orientation Committee meetings, and I attend weekly Orientation Roundtable Meetings.
  • Create and update the schedule of events, along with the Logistics Coordinator.
  • Am responsible for creation and maintenance of all publications, along with the Publications Coordinator.
  • Aide in overseeing the Committee's finances, along with the Finance Coordinator.
  • Ensure positive relations with all bodies concerned with Orientation Week, and ensure that the Orientation Committee meets all regulations and deadlines as set by the COMPSA Orientation policy manual and any governing bodies associated with Orientation Week.
